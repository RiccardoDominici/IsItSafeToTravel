**Environment (15%):** Measures natural hazard exposure and environmental health risks. Indicators: World Bank Air Pollution (PM2.5), INFORM Natural Hazard Risk, INFORM Climate Risk. Low score = high exposure to natural disasters, severe air pollution, climate-related hazards. High score = low natural hazard risk, good air quality.
